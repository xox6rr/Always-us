Shayris

1. Aapki muskurahat hi meri jaan hai,
Har din aapke saath hi armaan hai,
Aapke bina sab kuch veeran hai,
Aapka saath hi mere liye poori pehchaan hai,
Bas dua hai ye khuda se har roz,
Aapke pyaar me hi meri zindagi mehmaan hai. 🌷🫠
2. Aapki baatein hain sabse haseen,
Aapke bina kuch bhi nahi kareen,
Aapke saath hi roshni mili,
Har pal aapne khushiyaan bhari,
Bas dua karta hoon dil se yahi,
Aapka saath humesha rahe sada hi. 🌷🫠
3. Aapke khayalon me kho jana chahta hoon,
Aapke pyaar me jeena aur marna chahta hoon,
Aapke saath hi meri manzil hai,
Aap hi meri sabse azeez saahil hai,
Aap hain to jeevan hai poora mera,
Aapse juda koi armaan nahi mera. 🌷🫠
4. Aapke bina ye dil bechain rehta hai,
Aapke saath hi har sapna sach lagta hai,
Aapki hifazat me hai mere armaan,
Aapki muskurahat pe hai mere imaan,
Har waqt aapka saath chahiye mujhe,
Aap hi meri duaon ka rang laate hain. 🌷🫠
5. Aapki aankhon me hai meri zindagi,
Aapke saath hai meri khushnaseebi,
Aapke chehre se hai subah meri,
Aapki baton se shaam meri,
Aap hain to sab kuch hai mere liye,
Aapse hi hai har khushi mere liye. 🌷🫠
6. Aapke saath jo lamhe bitaye,
Woh yaadon me sada mehkaaye,
Aapke bina sab kuch adhoora hai,
Aapka pyaar hi bas poora hai,
Aap hain to jeevan me rang hai,
Aapka saath hi sabse bada dhang hai. 🌷🫠
7. Aapke hone se hai dil ko qarar,
Aapke saath hi milta hai pyar,
Aapke bina sab suna suna hai,
Aapke saath hi sapna sajna hai,
Bas aapki hansi hi meri shaan hai,
Aapke saath hi meri pehchaan hai. 🌷🫠
8. Aapki muskurahat se roshni hai,
Aapke pyaar se hi zindagi hai,
Aapka saath mere liye dua hai,
Aapke bina kuch bhi adhura hai,
Aap hain to sab kuch pyara hai,
Aapke saath hi bas sahara hai. 🌷🫠
9. Aapke saath chalna hai har mod pe,
Aapke pyaar me jeena hai har dor pe,
Aapki baton me hai sukoon mera,
Aapke pyaar me hai junoon mera,
Har din bas aapka saath chahiye,
Har pal bas aap hi ka pyaar chahiye. 🌷🫠
10. Aapka pyaar hai rehmat meri,
Aapka saath hai himmat meri,
Aapke saath hi jeevan sanvara hai,
Aapke pyaar me hi sab kuch guzara hai,
Aap hain to har din hai khushiyon se bhara,
Aapke bina dil rehta hai tanha tanha. 🌷🫠
11. Aapke saath jeevan me khushiyaan hai,
Aapke saath hi meri sab arziyaan hai,
Aapki har baat me hai pyar chhupa,
Aapka pyaar hi meri jaan ka sahara,
Har waqt bas aapka saath chahiye,
Aapse hi meri zindagi ka raasta saaf hai. 🌷🫠
12. Aapke pyaar se sajti hai meri duniya,
Aapke sapno se sajta hai mera jeevan,
Aapke hone se hai sab kuch roshan,
Aap hi ho har din ka mausam,
Aap hain to har raat bhi suhani hai,
Aapka saath hi meri nishani hai. 🌷🫠
13. Aapke saath guzre lamhe yaadgar hain,
Aapke bina sab kuch bekaar hai,
Aapki muskurahat se hi hai bahaar,
Aapka pyaar hi hai sabse pyara izhaar,
Bas aapke saath hi jeevan ki raah hai,
Aap hi meri khushiyon ki chaah hai. 🌷🫠
14. Aapke saath har din hai naya,
Aapke saath har dard hai gaya,
Aapke pyaar me hai sab kuch mehsoos,
Aap hain to dil hai bilkul loose,
Aapka saath mere liye barkat hai,
Aap hi meri ibadat hai. 🌷🫠
15. Aapke saath hi har subah suhani lage,
Aapke saath hi har shaam meherbaan lage,
Aapki hansi se saja hai mera jahaan,
Aapke pyaar se hai mera armaan,
Bas aapka saath hi chahiye sada,
Aapki yaadon me hi jeevan basaa. 🌷🫠
16. Aapki baton me hai sukoon ka asar,
Aapke pyaar me hai dil beqarar,
Aapka chehra hi meri tasveer hai,
Aapka saath hi meri taqdeer hai,
Bas aapke saath hi rehna hai mujhe,
Aap hi meri har dua me hai bas. 🌷🫠
17. Aapke pyaar me har din naya hai,
Aapke saath hi jeevan saja hai,
Aapki baaton me hai jadoo kuch aisa,
Aapka pyaar bana sab kuch pyara,
Bas aapke saath hi poora hai sapna,
Aapse juda ho na kabhi apna. 🌷🫠
18. Aapke saath hi har mod hai asaan,
Aapke saath hi har dukh hai anjaan,
Aapke bina kuch bhi nahi hai mera,
Aap hi hain jeene ka sahara mera,
Bas aapka pyaar hi chahiye sada,
Aapke saath hi hai meri har ibadat sada. 🌷🫠
19. Aapke saath hi meri muskurahat hai,
Aapke saath hi meri chaahat hai,
Aapke pyaar me hi hai jeevan me rang,
Aap hi hain mere sapno ka sang,
Bas aapka saath hi chahiye mujhe,
Aapse juda kabhi ho na mujhe. 🌷🫠
20. Aapke bina sab kuch adhoora hai,
Aapka saath hi sab kuch poora hai,
Aapki hansi me hai meri jaan basi,
Aapke pyar me hai har khushi hansi,
Bas aapka saath hi mera junoon hai,
Aap hi meri zindagi ka sukoon hai. 🌷🫠
21. Aapke saath hi hai mere sapno ka ghar,
Aapke pyaar me hi hai har safar,
Aapke bina kuch bhi nahi socha,
Aap hi hain mera sabse bada socha,
Bas aapka saath hi chahiye mujhe,
Aap hi meri zindagi me roshni hai bas. 🌷🫠
22. Aapke saath har dukh bhi lagta hai halka,
Aapke pyaar me har pal hai chamka,
Aap hi hain mere dil ki aas,
Aapke bina lagta hai sab kuch udaas,
Bas aapke saath hi poora hoon main,
Aap hi meri har subah hoon main. 🌷🫠
23. Aapki hansi me basa hai jahaan mera,
Aapke pyaar me hai armaan mera,
Aapka saath hi sab kuch bana,
Aapke bina lagta hai sab kuch suna,
Bas aapka pyar hi chahiye sada,
Aapse juda kabhi ho na yeh wada. 🌷🫠
24. Aapke saath jeevan hai itna pyara,
Aapke bina sab kuch bekaara,
Aapki baton me hai har sukoon,
Aapka pyaar hi hai mera junoon,
Bas aapke saath hi rehna hai mujhe,
Aap hi hain har dua me mujhe. 🌷🫠
25. Aapke saath hai meri poori duniya,
Aapke pyaar me hi hai meri bas khushiyaan,
Aapke bina sab kuch lagta hai veeran,
Aapka saath hai meri pehchaan,
Bas aapka pyar hi jeevan ki aas hai,
Aap hi meri har saans hai. 🌷🫠
